The Brown Bear

The brown bear is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America. It is one of the most widespread bear species and is known for its impressive strength and adaptability.

Learn more about the brown bear on Wikipedia.

Key Characteristics

  1. Thick fur for insulation
  2. Powerful limbs and claws
  3. Omnivorous diet

Natural Habitats

  • Forests
  • Mountains
  • Tundras
